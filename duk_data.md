# Digital University of Kerala (DUK) Admission 2025


## General Information About Digital University Kerala (DUK)

- Digital University Kerala (DUK), formerly known as Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management-Kerala (IIITM-K), was established in 2000 by the Government of Kerala as an autonomous institution. 
- The university offers postgraduate and doctoral programmes with focus areas including Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Internet of Things, Electronic Systems and Automation, Imaging Technologies, Data Analytics and Big Data, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, Ecological Informatics, Geospatial Analytics, and Applied Materials.

**Vision**
To become a globally reputed institution in digital education and research and to nurture future-ready talent capable of developing innovative and sustainable solutions for industry, government, and society.

**Mission**
The mission of the university is to “Curate a Responsible Digital World.”  
Core thematic pillars guiding academic programmes, research, training, and technology initiatives include:
- Computing
- Intelligence
- Sustainability
- Entrepreneurship

**Eligibility for Doctor of Philosophy (phD)**
- A Master’s degree of two years or more with a minimum of 60% marks  
  **OR**

- The qualifying degree must be in a discipline relevant to the chosen school

**For Final Year Candidates**
- Candidates who have appeared for or are appearing in the final examination of their qualifying degree are eligible to apply
- Such candidates must meet the minimum eligibility criteria by the date specified by the university

**Industry Regular PhD**
- Applicants must be full-time employees in an industry at the time of application
- Relevant full-time work experience after the Bachelor’s degree is required


## Minimum Eligibility for All Postgraduate Programmes

**Minimum Required Marks**
- A minimum of **60% marks** in the qualifying degree
  OR
- A **CGPA of 6.5 or higher** on a 10-point scale

- Marks will **not be rounded off** to meet eligibility requirements
- Candidates must submit an **official CGPA-to-percentage conversion certificate** issued by their university at the time of admission

**Relaxation for Reserved Categories**
- Relaxation in minimum qualifying marks is applicable as per university norms
- Candidates belonging to Other Backward Classes – Non-Creamy Layer (SEBC-NCL) are eligible for relaxation, provided they have passed the qualifying examination

**Final Year / Semester Students**
Candidates who have appeared for, or are currently appearing for, their final year or final semester examinations may apply if:
- They are appearing **for the first time** (no re-appear attempts)
- All previous semester/year examinations have been successfully passed

**Verification of Documents**
- Selected candidates must submit original mark sheets, degree/provisional certificates, and migration certificates within the specified deadline
- Failure to submit required documents may result in withdrawal of admission


## Admission Process

**M.Sc Programmes**
- Applicants must appear for the Digital University Admission Test (DUAT)
- DUAT is conducted for admission to various postgraduate programmes at the university

**M.Tech Programmes**
Admission is conducted through:
- National-level entrance examinations:
  - CUET (PG) – 2025
  - Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE)
- School-specific admission procedure:
  - Written online or offline test conducted by the university
  - Interview for shortlisted candidates
- Candidates with a valid GATE score will be directly called for the interview

**Master of Business Administration (MBA)**
- Admission requires a valid score in one of the following national-level exams:
  - CUET (PG) – 2025 (NTA)
  - CAT
  - CMAT
  - KMAT
  - XAT
  - NMAT by GMAC
  - GRE
- Shortlisted candidates must attend an interview

**Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)**
- Applicants must take the Digital University Research Aptitude Test (DRAT)
- Candidates with fellowships or scholarships through approved national-level exams are exempted from DRAT, including:
  - UGC-NET
  - UGC-CSIR NET
  - GATE
  - CEED
  - or other equivalent tests approved by the university

## DUAT Entrance Test – General Information and Syllabus

DUAT (Digital University Aptitude Test) is the internal entrance exam for Digital University Kerala.
It is required for certain postgraduate programmes (such as MBA, MSC Data Science, etc.)
and each programme has a different DUAT test paper code and syllabus.

**DUAT – Mode of Conduct**
- Conducted online
- AI-driven proctoring + human monitoring
- Laptop or desktop required
- 60 MCQs
- Duration: 60 minutes

**DUAT – Marking Scheme**
- +1 mark for each correct answer
- −0.25 negative marking per wrong answer


**DUAT Entrance Test - Syllabus by Programme**

**MBA**
- General Aptitude (20 marks): Grammar, Logic, Reasoning
- Mathematics (20 marks): Algebra, Calculus, Probability
- Reading Comprehension (20 marks)

**M.Sc Computer Science / Data Analytics / Computational Science**
- General Aptitude (20 marks)
- Mathematics (20 marks): Calculus, Vectors, Coordinate Geometry
- Computer Basics (20 marks): CPU, Boolean Algebra, Memory, I/O

**M.Sc Electronics and M.Sc Applied Physics**
- General Aptitude (20 marks): Verbal aptitude, Quantitative aptitude, Analytical aptitude
- Mathematics and Statistics (10 marks): Calculus, Probability & Statistics, Probability distributions, Correlation & Regression
- Core Subject Knowledge (30 marks): Electronics fundamentals, Devices & Circuits, Digital & Analog Electronics

**M.Sc Ecology and M.Sc Environmental Science**
- General Aptitude (20 marks): Verbal aptitude, Quantitative aptitude, Analytical aptitude
- Mathematics and Computer Basics (10 marks): Elementary Math, Computer basics, Common software tools
- Subject Knowledge (30 marks): Environmental Physics & Chemistry, Ecology, Sustainability topics

**M.Sc Data Science and Bio-AI**
- General Aptitude (20 marks)
- Mathematics and Statistics (10 marks): Permutations, Probability, Statistics
- Bioinformatics & Biological Sciences (10 marks)
- Chemistry & Physics Foundations (10 marks)
- Computer Basics (10 marks)

**M.Sc Data Science and Geoinformatics**
- General Aptitude (20 marks)
- Mathematics and Statistics (10 marks): Statistics, Probability, Independence of events, Elementary Data Analysis
- Earth and Environmental Sciences (20 marks): Earth systems, Natural resources, Agriculture, Biological & Power resources in India
- Computer Basics (10 marks)

## Hostel and Mess Facilities
The university provides hostel accommodation and mess services primarily for female students, with facilities focused on safety, hygiene, and accessibility.

**Hostel Accommodation**
- On-campus hostel facilities are available **only for female students**
- Hostel accommodation is provided in **twin-occupancy rooms**
- The hostel is a **six-storey building**
- For students who do not secure on-campus hostel allotment, the university will **assist in finding suitable accommodation alternatives**

**Safety and Security**
- Female warden available
- 24/7 round-the-clock security for both male and female students
- Fire protection system in place
- Substation and diesel generator (DG) backup for uninterrupted power supply

**Hygiene and Amenities**
- Bathroom facilities available on each floor
- Abundant water supply
- Sewage treatment plant
- Washing machines available on each floor

**Hostel Fees**
- Refundable caution deposit: **Rs. 6,000**
- Hostel fee per student per semester (twin occupancy): **Rs. 30,000**
- Mess fee: **As per actuals**

**Payment Terms**
- Hostel fee for the **first semester** must be paid **in advance at the time of admission**
- Hostel fee for **subsequent semesters** is payable at the **beginning of each semester**
